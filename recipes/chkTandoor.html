Chicken Tandoor

Chicken Tandoor is a classic North Indian dish where chicken is marinated in yogurt and a rich blend of spices, then roasted in a traditional clay oven (tandoor) or on a grill/oven at home. The result is smoky, juicy chicken with a charred exterior and tender inside, infused with flavors of garlic, ginger, and aromatic spices.

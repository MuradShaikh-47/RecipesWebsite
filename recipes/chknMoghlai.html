Chicken Moghlai

Chicken Mughlai is a rich and royal curry dish from the kitchens of the Mughal emperors. Known for its creamy, mildly spiced, and aromatic flavor, it combines tender chicken pieces with a silky gravy made from onions, cashews, yogurt, cream, and aromatic spices. Unlike fiery curries, Mughlai dishes are balanced, luxurious, and fragrant, often finished with a touch of saffron or kewra water. Served with naan, roti, or rice, Chicken Mughlai is a perfect example of Mughlai cuisineâ€™s blend of richness and subtlety.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Chicken

  • Ground Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare Nut Paste
  2. Fry Onions
  3. Cook Chicken
  4. Add Spices & Yogurt
  5. Make Mughlai Gravy
  6. Finish with Cream & Flavor
  7. Serve

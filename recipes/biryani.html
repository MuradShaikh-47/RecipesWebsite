Biryani


Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish that combines long-grain basmati rice with tender pieces of meat, vegetables, or seafood, layered with a rich blend of aromatic spices. Cooked slowly to perfection, the dish carries the essence of saffron, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, creating a mouthwatering aroma that fills the air even before it reaches the plate. Each spoonful offers a delightful balance of textures—the softness of rice, the juiciness of marinated meat, and the subtle crunch of fried onions or nuts. Often served with cooling raita, tangy pickles, or a fresh salad, biryani is more than just a meal—it is a celebration of culture, tradition, and the art of cooking, enjoyed across India and beyond in countless regional variations.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken / Mutton
  • Ghee
  • Yogurt
  • Whole Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare the Rice
  2. Marinate the Meat
  3. Fry Onions
  4. Cook the Meat
  5. Layer the Biryani
  6. Dum Cooking (Steaming)
  7. Serve
